Displaying ACLs

Displays the list off all the ACLs already configured for Trustees

  • Click on "Add" to add a new path (direcory or file) for which you want to manage specific access right. You may fill in this path manually, or by selecting a path in the server's filesystem tree. To do this, click on the "..." button located to the left of the "Add" button. This will open a new window in which you will be able to go through the filesystem tree. Once your choice has been made, click on the "OK" button. The selected path will be displayed in the text field.

  • Click on a path to manage its access rights. You then enter edit mode (see the edit page help section)

  • You can delete an access rule by clicking on the "delete" button on the right of the corresponding line. This line will be deleted from the Linux Trustees configuration file. Il you merely want to deactivate a rule without deleting it from the configuration file, click on the "Deactivate" button. After reloading the page, the corresponding link will be deactivated, and the background color will show its new state. To reactivate a deactivated line, click on tne "Activate" button. The deactivated link will become active, and the line's background color will be back to normal.
