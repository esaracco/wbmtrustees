Removing ACLs

Physically or logically removing an ACL

  • You can delete an access rule by clicking on the "delete" button on the right of the corresponding line. This line will be deleted from the Linux Trustees configuration file. Il you merely want to deactivate a rule without deleting it from the configuration file, click on the "Deactivate" button. After reloading the page, the corresponding link will be deactivated, and the background color will show its new state. To reactivate a deactivated line, click on tne "Activate" button. The deactivated link will become active, and the line's background color will be back to normal.
