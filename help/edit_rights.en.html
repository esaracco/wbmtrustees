Attributes and modifiers

Explanatory table

  • The meaning of the attributes and modifiers is as follows:
AttributeMeaning
RReading files
WWriting files and directories
BGoing through directories
EReading directories
XFile execution
UUse Unix permissions
Modifiers
CReinitialise persmissions (instead of defining them)
DDeny (instead of allow)
!The attribute applies to all but the specified owner (group or user)
OOne level only. The attributes applies to the specified directory and to the files it contains, but not to sub-directories
